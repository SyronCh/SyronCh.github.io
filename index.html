The Cast Must Die

A Multiplayer Survival Horror game made with Unreal Engine 5

About the Game

The Cast Must Die is a multiplayer survival horror game inspired by titles like "Five Nights at Freddy's" by Scott Cawthon, "The Joy of Creation" by Nikson, and "Five Nights at Freddy's Doom" by Skornedemon. Players must work together to survive against terrifying animatronic enemies in a haunted environment.
